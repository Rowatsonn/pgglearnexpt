Part Two

During this part of the study, each round you will be given 10 lab points and you must decide how much of this, if any, to donate to a public fund. This public fund will then be doubled and split evenly amongst all players. Then, anything that you earn from the public fund, as well as any money you did not donate to the pot will be added to your overall score. At the end of the game, all players will receieve a bonus payout determined by their score during the game.

Before proceeding, there will be two examples to help you understand how the game works which will explain how each participant will score depending on their behavior.
